The is a snake game modified by Yuekai Wu 13113181

Please use your keyboard four arrow keys to control snake

Red apple will increase your snake length and speed, try 
to get your highest score. Snake gains one life when you
get every ten scores

Yellow apple will increase snake lives and some speed, watch
out snake life.

Blue apple will reduce your snake speed but also snake lives

If you hit snake bodies or wall,the game will be over and
your highest score will be recorded.

If snake life is 0, the game will be over and your highest
score will be recorded

Hint: try to eat more red apple to get higher score, but snake's
speed is fast. Then you can eat some blue apples to reduce speed,
but watch out snake's lives.

Hint: yellow apple can provide extra live and lower speed
increasing.
